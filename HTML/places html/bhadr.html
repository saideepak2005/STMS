The Beauty of Kuntala Waterfalls

Located in the Adilabad district, Kuntala Waterfalls is the tallest waterfall in Telangana, dropping from a height of 200 feet. It is named after Shakuntala, the beloved wife of King Dushyanta, who is believed to have bathed in the waters here.
