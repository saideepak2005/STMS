The Story Behind the Fort

Built during the 12th century by the Kakatiya dynasty, Golconda Fort later became the capital of the Qutb Shahi dynasty. Known for its impressive architecture, acoustic wonders, and historic significance, the fort once housed the famed Koh-i-Noor diamond.
