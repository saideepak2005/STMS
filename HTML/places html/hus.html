HUSSAIN SAGAR LAKE

A Breathtaking Urban Lake with a Majestic Buddha Statue

A Timeless Beauty

Built in 1563 by Ibrahim Quli Qutb Shah, Hussain Sagar Lake is one of the largest artificial lakes in Asia. The serene waters connect Hyderabad and Secunderabad, offering breathtaking views and mesmerizing sunset cruises.
